How to change the user language

Changing the interface language

To personalize your experience, Fonectrix offers several regional language options. Follow these steps to update your settings:

  1. Visit www.fonectrix.info and be sure you are on the Index page.
  2. Click on SETTINGS at the top of the page.
  3. In the Application Settings area, look for the Regional Language option.
  4. Choose from one of the available language buttons:
  • English (default)
  • Français (CA)
  • Português
  • 繁體中文
  • 한국어 (Korean)
  • Русский (Russian)

Upon choosing your desired language, the website interface will automatically update to reflect your selection.
