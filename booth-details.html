How to find details for a booth

1. Find a Phone

To begin searching for a phone booth, visit the Find a Phone page, or select a region from the drop menu.

Go to payfonemap.com, click on the Find a Phone page, and select your desired country/region.

2. Select the booth

In the Locator Map menu, click on the booth from the left sidebar, or click on the coloured icon corresponding to the desired phone booth.

Tap or click on the payphone icon on the map, or select from the left sidebar.

3. Phone Booth Data

After selecting the desired phone booth, a box will appear displaying the following information about the booth:

  • Location/address
  • Telecom provider/operator
  • Photos (if available)
  • Additional notes about location or related info
